Step 02: Make the tea

To prepare the tea, you will need to follow the procedure below:

  1. Skill 1: Brewing the tea
    Boil water and pour it over the teabag. Wait 3 to 5 minutes for the tea to brew, without stirring or squeezing the teabag, and then remove the teabag.

  2. Skill 2: Adding milk
    After removing the teabag, pour in a dash of milk. Note: milk should act as only a flavor-enhancer. Too much milk will mask the taste of the tea.

After finishing the pearls and the milk tea, we can finally mix them together. Yay!
