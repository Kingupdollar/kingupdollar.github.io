Step 03: Serve the boba tea

Now that we have the boba milk tea, we may serve it and enjoy...not so fast! You will need to be careful of some detals before serving it to the table:

  1. Skill 1: Adding ice
    Having the boba tea in room temperature is totally a cult! Never try to do that. It is not an authentic way to have boba tea.

  2. Skill 2: Adding a wide straw
    Sucking the pearls with the straw is an indespensable experience while having boba tea. Thus, remember to use a wide straw!

Disclaimer: Above-mentioned tips to drink boba tea are my own personal insist. Feel free to try another method. Maybe you would discover Boba Tea: 3D The Experience.
