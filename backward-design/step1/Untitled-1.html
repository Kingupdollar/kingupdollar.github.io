Step 01: Make the pearl boba

To prepare the pearl boba, you will need to follow the procedure below:

  1. Skill 1: Boiling the pearls
    Add the pearls into and boil them. Stir until they begin to float, and then turn the heat down to medium and cook for 40 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  2. Skill 2: Sugar coating
    Cool down and drain pearls, and mix them in the honey and fully coat. Set aside for 5 minutes.
